Welcome to Let's Share

Let's Share is a social network platform designed for sharing personal experiences.
You will now complete an online group task as a trial run for the platform.
The computer will randomly pair you with other people.

First, all participants in the online task will create usernames, select profile pictures,
and write short posts to share with each other.

Then, you will spend 3 minutes with the other people in your group.
During this time, you will be able to read and react to each others' posts.

More instructions will follow.

Thank you!

Group Introduction

Soon after connecting, you should be able to see the other people's profiles.
You can read their posts and react to what they wrote about themselves by
clicking the “like” icon ♥, similar to how you would “like” a post on Instagram,
Facebook, Twitter, or TikTok.

Even though your interaction is minimal, please try to form an impression of the
people in the group. Try to imagine them in real life - how they might socialize with others,
what kind of people they are, or how you would get along with them.

The task will last 3 minutes and it is very important that you pay attention to the task.
Some questions about the task will follow.
